About Us

What is Communifree?

Commifree is an app where people can plan and attend events. Unlike other apps, Communifree allow individuals to create events instead of big groups. Our mission is to help people create authentic and lifelong friendships.

Who is Communifree for?

Communifree is for all adults looking to meet new people or exploring new hobbies. They can range from someone moving to a new city and looking for new friends to a grandma wanting to host a knitting party.
